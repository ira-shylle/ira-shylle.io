About this project


A friend of the founder of this online business approached me to help with redesigning the brand. The goal was to create a logo, templates for their social media posts, and some help with packaging design.


The idea for the logo was to improve what they already had as to not steer too far from the brand that people might have been familiar with already (old logo on the left). I started sketching ideas and the one on the right is what I eventually went with. I used Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to make the rough draft. To make it more personal, the client sent a picture of her daughter and I tried to make the illustration on the logo look like her.
