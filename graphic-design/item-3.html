About this project


In my Youtube thumbnail portfolio, I showcase my ability to help clients capture their viewers' attention with striking and compelling graphics. I take each thumbnail as an opportunity to tell a story and convey the message of the video to its audience. My current focus is on creating custom thumbnails for business mini-documentaries, ranging from success stories to dirty secrets, politics, and downfalls. My work is a playful and visually appealing representation of the message, but I'm highly versatile in design and ensure that I understand each client's aesthetic to deliver results that exceed their expectations.





This Youtube thumbnail features the young billionaire Palmer Luckey, with a creative twist on his famous electrocution story. In the design, he appears to be electrocuted by a coin with Mark Zuckerberg's face on it, a nod to the turning point of his career when Mark purchased Oculus for 2 billion dollars. The scattered money and Oculus symbolize his most notable achievements, while the Hawaiian shirt, a staple of Luckey's, adds a playful touch to the design. The drones flying in the background and the stamps on his face represent his current venture with Anduril Industries, a defense company that builds innovative weapons and surveillance equipment. The word "Cancelled" on the stamps alludes to the controversy that caused him to lose the support of VR game developers and eventually his job at Facebook. This thumbnail demonstrates the thought and dedication I put into each design to ensure the best possible output for my clients.
